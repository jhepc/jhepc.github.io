Description¶

The image depicts a Runge-Kutta ODE solver against a time adaptive Runge-Kutta ODE solver, as applied to the Lorenz equations. The corresponding IPython notebook is attached that produced the figure. The motivation for this work was to highlight the differences between these solvers during a homework in a nonlinear dynamics course that I am currently taking.
